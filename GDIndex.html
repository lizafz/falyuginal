Description

This catalog is a collection of images. The brief was to choose a category of words. (food, cloth, sounds, places, etc.) I have chosen places. In this catalog presented all the places mentioned in several chapters in a detective novel. Numbers under every image is a cipher that can be decode on the last spread. Image selection based on the number of the word in a sentence, the image is on the same place in Google search.
